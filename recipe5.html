Apple and Banana Smoothie Recipe

(serves 1)


Ingredients


1/2 cup of pure orange juice

1/4 cup low fat milk

1 whole banana, chopped into 4 pieces

1 whole apple, peeled and chopped into 4 pieces


Method


Place all ingredients in the blender, blend for approximately 20 seconds. Serve in a glass.
